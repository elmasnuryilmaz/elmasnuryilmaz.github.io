01 — About

Wet-lab and computational biology

I studied molecular biology and genetics at İTÜ, did my MSc in neurogenetics at IBG (Dokuz Eylül) with a research visit to Newcastle University, and I'm now completing my PhD at Ege University.

My work spans both sides of the bench: I run the wet-lab experiments and build the RNA-seq pipelines that analyze them. My main focus is post-transcriptional regulation — how TDP-43 shapes splicing and RNA processing in neurodegeneration.

Earlier, I worked on rare-disease genomics, using whole-exome sequencing to identify pathogenic variants in consanguineous families.
